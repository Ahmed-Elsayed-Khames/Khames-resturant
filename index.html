Khames resturant history

More than +16 years of experince

Khames Restaurant has been serving authentic Egyptian and Italian cuisine for over 16 years. Known for its rich flavors and warm atmosphere, Khames blends traditional recipes with modern culinary techniques to create a unique dining experience. From hearty Egyptian stews to classic Italian pastas, every dish is crafted with passion and precision. Whether you're looking for a quick bite or a full-course meal, Khames offers a taste of both worlds, promising quality and satisfaction in every bite.
